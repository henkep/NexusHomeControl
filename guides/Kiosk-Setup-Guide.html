What is Kiosk Mode?

Kiosk mode locks your tablet to display only the NEXUS dashboard. It prevents accidental exits, hides system notifications, keeps the screen always on, and automatically restarts the app if it crashes. This is perfect for wall-mounted smart home control panels.

7. Mounting & Power Solutions

Wall Mounting Options

Option 1: Magnetic Mount (Removable)

Best for: Renters or frequently moved tablets

  • Attach metal plate to tablet back (or case)
  • Mount magnetic base on wall with screws or adhesive
  • Tablet easily snaps on/off
  • Products: MagicMount, Scosche, iOttie

Cost: $20-40

Option 2: Flush Wall Mount

Best for: Permanent, clean installation

  • Tablet sits flush against wall in frame
  • Cable routed through wall
  • Professional appearance
  • Products: VidaMount, TabMount, Heckler Design

Cost: $50-150

Option 3: PoE Powered Mount

Best for: New construction or serious installations

  • Single Ethernet cable provides power + network
  • No WiFi needed, more reliable
  • Requires PoE switch or injector
  • Products: PoE Texas, Lavu, ArmorActive

Cost: $150-300 + PoE switch
